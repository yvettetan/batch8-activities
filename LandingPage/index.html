About Fix & Fogg

The name Fix & Fogg comes from the characters of the classic 1873 adventure novel, Around the World in 80 days by Jules Verne: Phileas Fogg and Detective Fix.Like the story, we hope to embark on a great journey of discovery, community and sustainability through our delicious, healthy and quality nut butter varieties.

Every jar of Fix & Fogg is made with love and care from start to finish in our factory in Wellington, New Zealand. From our humble little window in 2013, We’re really proud of the fact that our peanut butter has now won awards in New Zealand, the United States, Australia and Singapore - but even more so that people like you are out there supporting this journey that we're on and helping us grow as a business. You’re our people. We wouldn’t be able to do this without you. Although we're not quite sold in 80 countries, maybe one day we could do just that.

Roman & Andrea
